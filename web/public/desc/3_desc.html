vec_dot

Task

Create a function named vec_dot that returns the dot product of two vectors.
If the dimensions of the two vectors differ, your function must raise a ShapeMismatchedError.

Example

(234)⋅(567)=2⋅5+3⋅6+4⋅7=56 \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix} \cdot \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 6 \\ 7 \end{pmatrix} = 2\cdot5 + 3\cdot6 + 4\cdot7 = 56 ⎝⎛​234​⎠⎞​⋅⎝⎛​567​⎠⎞​=2⋅5+3⋅6+4⋅7=56

Test

a: vec = [2, 3, 4]
b: vec = [5, 6, 7]

assert vec_dot(a, b) == 56
